About Us

SPICE SOCIETY

Lets Make Tech Spicy

Society for Progression of Instrumentation and Control Engineering (SPICE) is a society which was established in the department of Instrumentation and Control Engineering (ICE), Dr. B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology, Jalandhar formed on December 1, 1994.

The society is continuously working for the welfare of students of Instrumentation and Control Engineering. A number of events have been organized under this society such as expert lectures from industry, workshops on robotics, career guidance & industrial tours.

OUR VISION And
MISSION

We aim to organise various technical competitions covering participation from students across the globe. We aim to build a strong mentorship cum guidance program, where juniors can be guided by their seniors in various technical domains and other work startegies.

To create and disseminate knowledge through research, quality education and creative inquiry.

To orient the education and research towards latest developments through close interactions with the industry, other institutions of higher learning and research organizations.

To organize workshops, short term courses, bootcamps for hands on experience in emerging fields and technology to students.
